Process

From Raw Materials to Premium Feed

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing process ensures consistent quality, optimal nutrition, and maximum digestibility.

1

Raw Material Collection

We source the finest quality ingredients from trusted suppliers:

  • Fish meal (high-quality protein source)
  • Soybean meal (plant-based protein)
  • Wheat and rice bran (carbohydrates)
  • Fish oil and vegetable oil (lipids)
  • Vitamins and mineral premixes
2

Quality Inspection

Every batch undergoes rigorous laboratory testing:

  • Protein content analysis
  • Moisture level verification
  • Contaminant screening (aflatoxins, heavy metals)
  • Freshness and odor assessment
  • Supplier certification verification
3

Processing & Grinding

Raw materials are precisely processed for optimal digestion:

  • Hammer mill grinding to specified mesh size
  • Particle size distribution control
  • Removal of foreign particles
  • Moisture adjustment for optimal pelleting
4

Mixing & Formulation

Computer-controlled mixing ensures precise nutrient ratios:

  • Automated batching with precise weighing
  • Homogeneous mixing for uniform distribution
  • Real-time monitoring of mixing time
  • Formula verification against specifications
5

Pellet Production

Extrusion at controlled temperature and pressure:

  • Steam conditioning for starch gelatinization
  • Controlled extrusion temperature (80-95°C)
  • Die selection for precise pellet size
  • Floating/sinking control via density adjustment
6

Packaging & Distribution

Final quality checks and proper packaging:

  • Final pellet quality inspection
  • Vacuum-sealed packaging preserves freshness
  • Proper labeling with batch number and date
  • Own fleet delivery for timely farm arrival
