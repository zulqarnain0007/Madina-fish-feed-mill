Our Farmers

Real people. Real farms. Real honest food.

We don't work with faceless suppliers. Every product on Purer Plate comes from a farmer we've shaken hands with, whose land we've walked on, and whose family we've shared tea with. Here are some of the incredible people who make honest food possible.

👨‍🌾 Farmer portrait
📍 Rajshahi

Abdul Malek

Specialty: Mangoes & Seasonal Fruits

Third-generation mango farmer from Chapainawabganj. Abdul grows 12 varieties of mangoes using traditional methods passed down from his grandfather. He refuses to use chemical ripeners, even when it means lower yields.

Langra Mango Himsagar Mango Fazli Mango
👩‍🌾 Farmer portrait
📍 Sylhet

Rokeya Begum

Specialty: Raw Honey & Bee Products

Rokeya manages 40 beehives in the tea gardens of Sylhet. Her honey is harvested only twice a year to ensure the bees have enough reserves. She practices sustainable beekeeping that protects local biodiversity.

Sylhet Raw Honey Honeycomb Beeswax
👨‍🌾 Farmer portrait
📍 Kushtia

Md. Hasan Ali

Specialty: Farm Butter & Ghee

Hasan's family has been making ghee the traditional way for over 80 years. He sources milk from 15 local dairy farmers who grass-feed their cows. His ghee is slow-cooked for 6 hours over a wood fire.

Cultured Ghee Farm Butter Clarified Butter
👩‍🌾 Farmer portrait
📍 Dinajpur

Parveen Akter

Specialty: Heritage Rice

Parveen preserves indigenous rice varieties that are at risk of disappearing. She grows Kalijira, Chinigura, and Kataribhog using zero-tillage methods that protect soil health and water tables.

Kalijira Rice Chinigura Rice Kataribhog Rice
👨‍🌾 Farmer portrait
📍 Khulna

Jahangir Hossain

Specialty: Natural Sweeteners

Jahangir produces date molasses (khejur er gur) and palm jaggery using solar-powered evaporation. His farm is fully organic and produces zero waste — every byproduct is used as animal feed or compost.

Date Molasses Palm Jaggery Date Sugar
👩‍🌾 Farmer portrait
📍 Cox's Bazar

Fatema Khatun

Specialty: Whole Spices

Fatema grows turmeric, ginger, and red chilies on terraced hillsides. She sun-dries everything naturally and grinds spices to order so they retain maximum flavor and potency.

Turmeric Powder Red Chili Ginger Powder
