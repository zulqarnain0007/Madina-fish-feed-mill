ABOUT US

Our Journey Started in April 2026

Madina Harvest is a natural farm located in the heart of Bangladesh - Trishal, Mymensingh. We started with 45 quail birds producing 40+ eggs daily. Our goal is to provide natural, high-quality protein sources including eggs, birds, chicken, goat, and cattle.

By the end of 2026, we aim to expand to 300-400 birds and introduce high-quality desi chicken farming for eggs and meat. We also sell organic products like pure mustard oil. All our partner farmers are contracted to use zero pesticides - 100% natural.
