Our Story

🌾 Fields at sunrise
How It Started

We Asked a Simple Question

In 2023, we stood in a Dhaka supermarket staring at a bottle of "pure honey" and wondered: Who made this? Where did it come from? What's actually in it?

We couldn't find answers. So we went to the source — small farms across Bangladesh where farmers still grow food the way their grandparents did. No shortcuts. No chemicals. Just soil, sun, and skill.

Purer Plate was born from that journey. We built a bridge between these honest farmers and conscious eaters who deserve to know exactly what they're putting on their plates.

What We Stand For

Radical Transparency

Three pillars that guide everything we do

🔍

Full Traceability

Every product has a story. Scan any item to see which farm it came from, who grew it, and when it was harvested. No secrets.

🧪

Lab Tested

We independently test every batch for pesticides, heavy metals, and adulteration. If it doesn't pass, it doesn't reach your plate.

💚

Fair to Farmers

Farmers set their own prices. We take a small commission and deliver the rest directly to them. No middlemen. No exploitation.
