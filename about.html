Our Story

Building Trust in Bangladesh Aquaculture

Madina Fish Feed Mill was established with a vision to revolutionize fish farming in Bangladesh. Located in the heart of Trishal, Mymensingh, we started as a small operation serving local farmers and have grown into one of the region's most trusted fish feed manufacturers.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every fish farmer deserves access to high-quality, affordable feed that maximizes yield while maintaining fish health. Today, we serve hundreds of farmers across Mymensingh and neighboring districts.

We combine traditional farming wisdom with modern nutritional science to create feed formulas perfectly suited to Bangladesh's climate, water conditions, and fish species.
